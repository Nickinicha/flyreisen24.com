Dear users,

flyreisen24.com values the protection of your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and other data you provide through forms on our site
  • Usage Information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on the website
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and personalize content
  • Travel Information: Travel routes, dates, and special requirements you specify

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide and improve your experience on our website
  • To fulfill your requests (e.g., flight searches)
  • To analyze usage and improve our services
  • To contact you about related services or offers, if you've given consent

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We work with the following third parties:

  • TravelPayouts: To provide flight search and comparison services.
  • Google Analytics: To analyze website usage and traffic.
  • GitHub Pages: To provide website hosting and file storage.
  • Cloudflare: To optimize network performance, speed, and security.
  • Formspree: To manage and process contact form inquiries and messages.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, data may be shared with partners only for service-related purposes.

4. International Data Transfers

Since we work with international service providers, your data may be stored and processed in countries outside your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data remains protected wherever it is processed.

5. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy or as required by law‚Äîtypically no more than 2 years.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access and receive a copy of your data
  • Right to correct or update your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@flyreisen24.com

7. Policy for Minors

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared personal data with us, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Email Marketing

If you've subscribed to our newsletters or promotions, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email or contacting us at info@flyreisen24.com

9. Legal Requests

In certain situations, we may disclose your personal data in response to legal orders, subpoenas, or other legal processes. We may also disclose data to protect our rights, your safety, investigate fraud, or comply with government requests.

10. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information and use appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will take effect when we post the revised version on our website. We encourage you to review the policy periodically.

12. Applicable Laws

We operate under the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and other relevant data protection laws. If you are in the EU, we respect your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
