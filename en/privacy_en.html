Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and other data you provide through our forms
  • Usage Information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience
  • Travel Information: Routes, dates, and special requirements you specify

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your requests (e.g., flight searches)
  • Analyze usage and improve our offerings
  • Contact you about related services or offers (with your consent)

3. Third-Party Sharing

We work with these third parties:

  • TravelPayouts: Flight search and comparison services
  • Google Analytics: Website usage analysis
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting
  • Cloudflare: Network performance and security
  • Formspree: Contact form processing

We don't sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

We retain your data as needed, typically no more than 2 years.

5. Your Rights

  • Right to access and receive a copy of your data
  • Right to correct your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@flyreisen24.com

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze website usage
  • Personalize content

You can refuse cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some website features.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

8. Contact Us

For questions about this policy:

Email: contact@flyreisen24.com
